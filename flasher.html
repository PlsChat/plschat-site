⚠️ Device stuck in boot loop?

Follow these steps exactly to force the device into download mode:

  1. Unplug the USB cable from the device.
  2. Hold down the BOOT button (small button next to USB-C).
  3. While still holding BOOT, plug the USB cable back in.
  4. Keep holding the BOOT button for 2–3 seconds, then release it.
  5. The device should now stop resetting and be ready for flashing.

After doing this, click Erase Device on this page, then try flashing again.
