Connecting to Your Device

How to reach the chat interface hosted on your device.

  1. 1 Power on your PLSChat device and wait about 10 seconds for it to start broadcasting WiFi.
  2. 2 On your phone or laptop, connect to the WiFi network PLSChat-XXXX (the last 4 characters come from your device's MAC — readable on the Configure Device page). Default password is pls12345 — for security, change both the WiFi and Bluetooth passwords before using the device in the field.
  3. 3 Open a browser and go to http://192.168.4.1
  4. 4 Choose the device's web UI password when prompted — this is what you'll use to unlock the chat interface from now on.
Your phone or laptop may warn "No internet connection" while joined to this network — that's expected. The device works fully offline, so stay connected to it rather than switching back to your normal WiFi.
