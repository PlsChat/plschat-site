Connecting to Your Device

How to reach the chat interface hosted on your device.

1
Power on your PLSChat device and wait about 10 seconds for it to start broadcasting WiFi.
2
On your phone or laptop, connect to the WiFi network PLSChat-XXXX. Default password is pls12345 — change it in Settings once logged in.
3
Open a browser and go to http://192.168.4.1
4
Enter your device PIN when prompted.

Your phone may warn "No internet connection" — that's expected. The device works fully offline.
