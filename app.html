Install

  1. Tap Download PLSChat.apk above.
  2. Open the downloaded file. Android (and GrapheneOS) will ask to allow installing from this source — approve it for your browser or files app.
  3. Open PLSChat, enter your device's hotspot password (default pls12345), and tap Connect automatically.

Made for GrapheneOS

On GrapheneOS and modern stock Android, a Wi-Fi network with no internet — like a PLSChat device's hotspot — is often refused or silently dropped. This app binds its own process directly to the PLSChat network via ConnectivityManager, so the device UI loads every time. No Google services, no extra dependencies, nothing phones home.

Why an app?

A PLSChat device serves its whole interface from its own Wi-Fi hotspot at 192.168.4.1. The app opens that interface in a native WebView over the bound connection — the same chat UI, made reliable on a phone.
