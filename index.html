We are the network.
No internet. No mobile towers.

PLSChat is a fully decentralised, encrypted text messaging system that runs on LoRa mesh networks. Your devices become the infrastructure.

Get Started Learn how it works
THE VISION

True decentralised
communication.

PLSChat lets people communicate without relying on the internet, mobile networks, or any central authority.

Messages travel directly from device to device over long-range LoRa radio. Your community becomes the network.

No Internet Required
Works completely offline. Perfect for remote areas, disasters, or when networks are censored.
We Are The Network
Every PLSChat device helps relay messages for others. The more devices, the stronger the network becomes.
End-to-End Encrypted
Messages are encrypted on your device. Only the intended recipient can read them.
WHY IT MATTERS

Communication that can't be switched off.

Works Anywhere

Mountains, forests, disaster zones, or areas with no mobile coverage. If two devices can hear each other, they can communicate.

Censorship Resistant

No central servers to shut down. Messages keep moving as long as devices are powered on and within range.

Community Powered

Run a device and you help strengthen the network for everyone. Earn rewards for keeping the mesh alive.

HOW IT WORKS

Simple. Powerful. Yours.

1
Flash a Device
Upload PLSChat firmware to any compatible LoRa device (Heltec, SenseCAP, etc).
2
Join the Mesh
Devices automatically discover each other and form a self-healing mesh network.
3
Send Messages
Use the web interface or mobile app to send encrypted messages across the mesh.
